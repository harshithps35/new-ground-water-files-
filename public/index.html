🔬 How GRACE Works for Groundwater
🌍
Gravity Measurement
GRACE-FO satellites detect minute variations in Earth's gravitational field caused by changes in water mass stored underground.
💧
TWS Anomaly
Terrestrial Water Storage anomaly = deviation from the 2004–2009 baseline. Negative = water depletion. Positive = recharge above baseline.
📊
Groundwater Isolation
Combining GRACE data with soil moisture models (GLDAS) isolates the groundwater storage component from surface water and soil moisture.
🤖
AI Downscaling
Machine learning downscales GRACE's ~300km resolution to local zone-level (1–5km) by fusing with IoT sensor ground truth data.

🏗 Recommended Structures
Percolation Ponds
Shallow excavations that allow rainwater to seep into the ground. Ideal for areas with sandy loam / red laterite soil. Capacity: 45,000–2,50,000 L.
Recharge Shafts
Vertical borings (30–60m deep) that bypass low-permeability layers to directly recharge deeper aquifers. ROI: 3 years.
Check Dams
Small barriers across seasonal streams to slow runoff and maximize infiltration into natural recharge zones. Best for rural belts near Nandi Hills.
Rooftop RWH
Collects rooftop rainwater and diverts to sump/borewell recharge pit. Mandatory for plots >200 sqm. Payback: 2 years.
